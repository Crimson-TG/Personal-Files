Odd and Even

What is Odd Number?

Odd numbers are the numbers that cannot be divided by 2 evenly. It cannot be divided into two seperate integers evenly. If we divide an odd number by 2, then it will leave a remainder. The examples of odd numbers are 1, 3, 5, 7, etc.

What is Even Number

Even number is any number that can be divided exactly by 2. Even number always end up with the last digit as 0, 2, 4, 6, or 8. Some examples of even numbers are 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 14, 16, etc.
