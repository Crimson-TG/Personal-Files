What Is Tobacco Use?

Tobacco use may be efine as any habitual use of the tobacco plant leaf and its products. The predominant use of tobacco is by smoke inhalation of cigarettes, pipes, an cigars. Smokeless tobacco refers to a variety of tobacco products that are neither sniffed, sucked, or chewed.


What Is Cannabis Use?

People use the marijuana in many different ways, such as: eating, smoking, drinking, vaping the THC extract, or inhaling it. The THC can result in many health problems and effects, which is dangerous in long term use of it.


What Is Vape Use?

Vape is an electronic device that stimulates the smoking of tobacco. Vape have an atomizer, battery for its power source, and a cartridge or tank that is filled by the liquid. The users only ihales the vapor and not the smoke, since vape doesn't contain a plant to burn like cigarettes, but rather it contain liquid to vaporize.
