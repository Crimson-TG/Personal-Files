My Hometown

Cuenca Batangas

Troy Gian A. Cadano

Growing up in my hometown was a wonderful experience. It was a small town with a close-knit community where everyone knew each other. I have many fond memories of my childhood there.

One of my favorite things to do was to visit the some local park and malls with my childhood friends; also those night park when there's fiesta. We also play tag, card/board games,. The park was also bustling with activity, and it was a great place to meet new people.

Overall, I learned so much from the people and the community around me. I hope to visit again soon and relive some of those memories, and i'm really looking forward for it.
